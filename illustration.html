illustration

Collectively we've come a long way from MS Paint, but digital illustration still holds my entire heart. Through the decades my style has shifted tremendously, but retro art has always been at the core of my aesthetic. These days, I work primarily in Pixsquare and Procreate for iOS.

I'm also partial to low-poly 3D modelling, the clunkier and more pixelated the better. PicoCAD, Blockbench, and Crocotile 3D are my tools of choice. Terminally averse to using the "proper" tools for the trade (I can't get through a Blender tutorial), working with limited software lends to the authenticity of my artistic vision.
