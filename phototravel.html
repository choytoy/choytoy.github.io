Spring in Mainland Japan (2024)

A collection of photographs from my 2024 trip to Japan. Photos are presented unedited, shot using Marumi's Alps Punch! (アルプスパンチ！) and Summer Punch! (なついろパンチ！) special effect filters, meant to evoke a feeling reminiscent of Japanese cinema in the 90s and early 00s.

Through this filter, Japan takes on an air of millennial memories, with intentionally low in-camera resolution and marked colour grading. These filters, designed by Suzuki Sayaka (鈴木さや香), photographer and owner of Atelier Piccolo, incorporate the nostalgic aesthetics of Japan's Heisei era (1989-2019).
